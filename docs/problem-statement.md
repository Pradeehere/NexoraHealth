# Nexora Health – Problem Statement

## Project Title
Nexora Health – AI Powered Health Tracker & Wellness Monitoring System

## Abstract
Modern lifestyles often lead to unhealthy habits, including poor diet, lack of exercise, and inadequate sleep. Nexora Health is designed to be an all-in-one health companion that not only tracks these metrics but uses AI to provide actionable wellness advice based on personalized data. By centralizing daily logs and mapping trends over time, users gain complete visibility into their well-being.

## Problem Being Solved
Many existing health tracking applications are either too fragmented (requiring multiple apps for sleep, diet, and exercise) or too complex. Users struggle to find a clean, unified interface that correlates their habits with their overall health. Moreover, most generic apps simply display numbers without providing intelligent, context-aware suggestions for improvement.

## Proposed Solution
Nexora Health provides a unified dashboard to monitor calories, sleep, hydration, BMI, and physical activity. Leveraging an AI suggestions endpoint, it analyzes user logs to provide custom health tips. It implements a fully responsive Progressive Web App (PWA) architecture for immediate access across any device.

## Scope
- Secure User Authentication (JWT)
- Daily logging of health records (calories, water, sleep, mood)
- Target goal setting and tracking
- Responsive Dashboard and specialized Reports area
- AI-driven suggestions
- Admin panel for system overview

## Expected Outcomes
Users will adopt healthier routines through active monitoring and tailored feedback, successfully hitting their targets while understanding their long-term health trends.

## Target Users
- Health-conscious individuals seeking a unified tracking system
- Professionals looking for a quick and simple way to monitor daily wellness stats
